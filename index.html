1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Mahaveer Palm Grove mobile application ("App", "we", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services.

By using the App you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App and discontinue use.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account information

When you register or log in, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Flat / unit number
  • Password (stored as a one-way hash — we never store your plaintext password)

2.2 Camera data

The App uses your device camera to capture images of DG meter readings for OCR processing. Images are transmitted to our backend solely to extract the meter reading value. We do not retain the raw images after processing.

2.3 Biometric data

Biometric authentication (fingerprint / face unlock) is processed entirely on your device by the operating system. We never receive, store, or transmit your biometric data.

2.4 Push notification token

With your permission, the App registers a device push token to deliver maintenance alerts, payment reminders, and community notices. You can revoke notification permission at any time in your device settings.

2.5 Usage and crash data

We collect anonymised crash reports and error logs to diagnose issues and improve stability. These logs do not include personally identifiable information.

2.6 Data we do NOT collect

  • Precise or approximate location
  • Contacts, calendar, or files on your device
  • Browsing history or data from other apps
  • Any data unrelated to the App's residential management functions

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticating your identity and securing your account
  • Processing DG meter readings and generating consumption records
  • Sending maintenance updates, payment dues, and community announcements
  • Responding to support requests
  • Diagnosing technical issues and improving App performance

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), password hashing, and access controls to protect your information.

Sensitive credentials on your device (e.g. session tokens) are stored in the platform's secure keystore (Android Keystore) and are not accessible to other applications.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request deletion of your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may process data on our behalf:

  • Expo (expo.dev) — mobile application framework and over-the-air update delivery. Expo may collect anonymised telemetry about runtime errors.
  • Railway (railway.app) — cloud infrastructure hosting our backend API. Data transmitted to the App passes through Railway's servers.
  • Razorpay (razorpay.com) — payment processing. When you make a payment or top up your wallet, you are redirected to a Razorpay-hosted payment page in your browser. Razorpay processes your payment details directly; we do not receive or store your card or UPI credentials. Razorpay's privacy policy applies to data you enter on their payment page.

We do not integrate with advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for adult residents and management staff. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have inadvertently done so, we will delete that information promptly.

8. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Withdraw consent for push notifications (also available in device settings)

We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
